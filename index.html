About Me

Emily Gerrity

Product Designer

I am a Product Designer with experience in creating design systems, overhauling existing products as well as designing new products. I always approach my product work from a user-first perspective while maintaining business interests. I am process-focused, and have a love of design systems. I also enjoy mentoring other designers in Figma best practices. My previous role was at a start up in the business travel space that had a really intelligent and close-knit design team. My role was focused on design system work and our mobile app. My responsibilities included Figma administration, user research, wire framing, prototyping, and developer hand off.
